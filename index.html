Life & Times

1853

Born in Groot Zundert, North Brabant, Netherlands

1888

Moves to Arles, France, and paints many of his most famous works

1889

Spends time at the Saint-Rémy-de-Provence asylum

1890

Dies in Auvers-sur-Oise, France

Enduring Legacy

Influence on Art

Van Gogh's expressive style and emotional intensity have profoundly influenced generations of artists, shaping the course of modern art.

Post-Impressionism Expressionism Fauvism

Artistic Techniques

Impasto

Thick application of paint, creating texture and depth.

Expressive Brushstrokes

Bold and dynamic brushstrokes conveying emotion and movement.

Vibrant Colors

Use of intense and contrasting colors to evoke strong feelings.

Complementary Colors

Strategic use of complementary colors to create visual contrast and harmony.

Global Influence

Art and Painting

Van Gogh's work continues to inspire artists worldwide, influencing various art movements and styles.

  • Post-Impressionism
  • Expressionism
  • Modern Art

Popular Culture

Van Gogh's image and art have permeated popular culture, appearing in films, music, and merchandise.

  • Movies and documentaries
  • Music videos and album art
  • Fashion and design

Museums and Collections

Van Gogh Museum, Amsterdam

Metropolitan Museum of Art, New York

Features several notable Van Gogh paintings.
